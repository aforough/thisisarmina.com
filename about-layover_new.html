I'm Armina, an interaction designer from Vancouver, currently finishing up my undergrad. In the past few years, I've had the opportunity of interning at Green Stone (design + strategy consultancy), In-it (social innovation startup), Philips, and Google.

Having grown up in a politically active family, I've developed a an interest in growing the spaces where social issues, critique, intersect with the world of design.

As a culturally aware designer, my goal is to address the kinds of people-problems that, in my own very opinionated and subjective view, are worth solving for. I'm also passionate about the innovation that drives emerging technology, but at the same time I remain critical of its ethical aspects and implementations.
